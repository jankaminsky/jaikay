Enjoy.

JAIKAY Creative's mantra is "enjoy." To each individual, the word "enjoy" is unique. But it also has a common meaning to everyone: to understand "enjoy," one must also understand what makes them feel the opposite — pain, discomfort, anxiety, suffering, and so forth.


Art made by JAIKAY taps into the duality of "enjoy" by representing both of its sides, its uniqueness and its commonality. It depicts the ancient philosophies of yin and yang, the path towards conquering the inner battle within yourself, and the appreciation of the moment — no matter the situation.


Influenced by urban style, JAIKAY is an artist based in Montréal, Québec, Canada. He started JAIKAY Creative in 2021 to exhibit art based in varying medias, both physical and digital.

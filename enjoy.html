Enjoy.

JAIKAY Creative's mantra is "enjoy." To each individiual, the word "enjoy" is unique. But it also has a common meaning to everyone: to understand "enjoy," one needs to first understand what makes them feel the opposite - pain, discomfort, anxiety, suffering, and so forth.


Art made by JAIKAY taps into the duality of "enjoy" by both of its sides, its uniqueness and commmonality. It depicts the ancient philosophies of yin and yang, the winning of the inner battle within yourself, and the appreciation of the moment you are in no matter your situation.


Influenced by urban style, JAIKAY is an artist based in Montréal, Québec, Canada. He started JAIKAY Creative in 2021 to exhibit art based in varying medias, both physical and digital.
